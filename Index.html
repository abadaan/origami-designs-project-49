Intresting Facts About Cicada

1. A Cicada is an insect of the order Hemiptera, suborder Auchenorrhyncha, in the superfamily Cicadoidea.

2. Cicadas are arranged into two families: Tettigarctidae and Cicadidae. There are two extant species of Tettigarctidae, one in southern Australia, and the other in Tasmania.

3.Adult cicadas, sometimes called imagines, are usually between 2 and 5 cm (1 to 2 inches) long. Although there are some tropical species that reach 15 cm (6 in).

Intresting Facts About Bears

1.Bears are extraordinarily intelligent animals.

2. Bears grieve deeply for others. Cubs are known to moan and cry when separated from their mothers.

3.Bears care deeply about family members. They will risk their lives and even fight to the death in order to save a cub or sibling from danger.
